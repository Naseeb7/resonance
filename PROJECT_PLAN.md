# Resonance

Resonance is a calm, emotionally supportive PWA for daily speech therapy practice.

The experience is inspired by Duolingo’s progression psychology, but redesigned specifically for speech therapy users working through vulnerability, confidence, and articulation challenges.

The prototype focuses on rhotacism (“R” sound) practice through short guided exercises, smooth transitions, and emotionally encouraging feedback.

---

# Core Product Principles

- Encouraging over punitive
- Calm over gamified
- Confidence-building over performance pressure
- Warm and clinically trustworthy
- Mobile-first and app-like
- Minimal and emotionally safe

---

# Lesson Flow

1. Welcome Screen
2. Listen & Repeat Exercise
3. Word Recognition Exercise
4. Mirror Mode Exercise
5. Reflection Check-In
6. Completion Screen

---

# Exercise Details

## Listen & Repeat

User listens to a word and performs a mocked recording interaction.

## Word Recognition

User identifies the strongest “R” sound from multiple options.

## Mirror Mode (Innovation)

Uses the front-facing camera to create a guided articulation awareness experience.

The purpose is not AI scoring.
The purpose is self-awareness, confidence, and physical speech practice support.

## Reflection Check-In

A short emotional reflection after practice.

This reinforces emotional safety and consistency over perfection.

---

# Completion Experience

The completion screen should feel rewarding but emotionally mature.

Avoid childish celebration patterns.

Preferred tone:

- “You showed up today.”
- “Consistency strengthens articulation.”
- “Every repetition builds muscle memory.”
